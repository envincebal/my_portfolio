About me

Hello! I am a full stack developer residing in Portland, OR. I am a graduate of CareerFoundry's Full Stack Development program, an intensive project-based course that taught me a wide range of best practices in web development. I am also an active participant of Free Code Camp. I love learning new technologies that allow me to build bigger and more complex web apps.

In my spare time, I love playing video games, reading, finding new local places to eat at and now I am learning how to cook.

SkillSet

My toolset goes beyond the basic front-end trinity of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I also use frameworks and libraries such as Bootstrap, Sass and React.js in my workflow. Currently, I am delving into the back-end using Node.js, MongoDB and Express.js to be more versatile as a developer. I always strive to create web experiences that are not just functional but also look good across all devices.
